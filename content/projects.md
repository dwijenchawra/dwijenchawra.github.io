### Soundscape 

[GitHub](https://github.com/dwijenchawra/soundscape) | Jan 2023

- Created an intelligent playlist generator that uses NLP and performs sentiment analysis on transcribed speech.
- Stored vectorized songs in CockroachDB’s relational database and generated playlists based on user emotion.
- Won “Best use of CockroachDB” at Purdue’s annual Boilermake X hackathon.

### FitFinder

[GitHub](https://github.com/dwijenchawra/helloworld2022) | Nov 2022
